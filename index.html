Rock Mechanics

Since 1981, Call & Nicholas, Inc. (CNI) has provided a wide range of engineering services to the mining industry. Located in Tucson, Arizona, our highly qualified engineers and scientists have extensive experience in the rock stability of open-pit and underground mines.
Our business is focused on finding the safest and most economically viable way of mining.

The CNI Method

Over the past 30 years, we have developed a sophisticated and comprehensive set of tools to assess the validity of a potential open-pit or underground mine design. Our methods primarily focus on a probabilistic approach. Compared to other methods, we can perfom stability analyses that honor the uncertainty which is naturally present in rock masses. The probabilistic method also allows for the selection of criteria that is based on an acceptable risk.
